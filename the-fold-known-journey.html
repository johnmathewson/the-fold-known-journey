Week 2
🚪
SAFE
What Are You Carrying?
"I am the gate; whoever enters through me will be saved." — John 10:9

The Gate Exercise

What are you carrying? Fears. Shame. Pain. Grief. Lay them at the cross.

✝️

Guided Prayer

"Jesus, I bring these things to your cross. I don't want to carry them anymore. I lay down every fear, every lie, every hurt. You said your yoke is easy and your burden is light. I trust you with all of it."

Matthew 11:28-30

Is there something you've been carrying that you didn't type? You don't have to write it here — but tell Jesus about it right now, silently.

Week 3
👂
CALLED
Learning to Listen
"My sheep hear my voice, and I know them, and they follow me." — John 10:27

How God Speaks

Guided Listening Prayer

Ask Jesus: "What do you want me to know about myself right now?"

3:00

What Did You Hear?

Write down what thoughts came to mind. Even if you're not sure it was God, write it down.

Tip: If you didn't hear anything, that's okay. Sometimes God speaks later — in a dream, through a friend, or through a verse. Keep your ears open this week.

Week 4
🧭
SENT
Living Bold
"I have other sheep that are not of this fold. I must bring them also." — John 10:16

"You will be my witnesses..." — Acts 1:8

Your Bold Step

This week, I will take ONE bold step for God:

Commissioning Prayer

"Jesus, I'm not just staying in the fold — I'm going out. You've shown me who I am. You've taken what I was carrying. You've spoken to me. Now I go. Give me courage. Give me words. Let someone see You through me this week. Amen."

Your Journey Recap

I am KNOWN by God — He saw me before I was born and calls me by name.
I am SAFE in His fold — the cross has taken everything I was carrying.
I am CALLED by His voice — my sheep hear my voice, and I know them.
I am SENT with His purpose — I have other sheep. I must bring them also.
This is who you are.
Go live like it.
